      The NMH Fund is a priority of This Place, This Moment: The Campaign for Northfield Mount Hermon. The NMH Fund is the primary means for all alums, parents, and friends to be a part of the campaign and help sustain the distinctive features that make NMH special. Thank you for your trust and investment in our enduring values of educating the head, heart, and hand!

      Gifts to the NMH Fund support financial aid, academics, athletics, community service programs, social activities, and much more.
      Your donation makes a difference for current students and faculty.
