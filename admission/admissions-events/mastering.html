Mastering the Application Process Series

Applying to boarding school takes time and effort. We're here to help.

We know that preparing to apply for a boarding school can feel like a daunting task. NMH’s admission counselors are ready to help you with the boarding school application process. Our team has a combined 100+ years of experience helping families apply for boarding school admissions applications  . We work with families like yours every day — and we love what we do.

First, take a look at our checklist for researching and comparing schools.

Then, check out our boarding high school application series of free, virtual “Mastering the Application: Tips” workshops, where our team will help you navigate, step by step, the process of researching, applying to, and deciding on the right school for you.

Upcoming Boarding School Application Workshops

INTERVIEWING: YOU'RE IMPRESSIVE - LET'S SHOW IT!

Date Offered:

  • Wednesday, October 1, 2025 | 8 am & 7 pm ET

  • Tuesday, December 2, 2025 | 8 am & 7 pm ET

Putting yourself out there in an interview is easy for some people. Others need time to warm up — and you don’t have that time during the interview. This session offers tips on how to make the interview go your way. Whether you’re outgoing or reserved, you have strengths, and you need the confidence to make them shine. This isn't bragging: It’s sharing who you are and why you should be at a school.

We'll cover:

  • Interview etiquette.
  • Feeling comfortable in an interview setting.
  • How to highlight personal strengths.
  • How to talk about weaknesses with a growth mindset.
  • Typical interview questions.
