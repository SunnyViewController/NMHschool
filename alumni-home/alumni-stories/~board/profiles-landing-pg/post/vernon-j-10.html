Vernon J ‘10

Vernon J ’10 is the founder of Equity Platforms, the first blockchain-based digital token backed by affordable housing. A seasoned real estate professional, Vernon has facilitated over $180 million in multifamily property transactions across the U.S. He is also a passionate community-builder and the co-founder of multiple impactful initiatives, including Healthy NMY, NY for Seniors, and EastNewYork.com — programs designed to uplift and empower underserved communities.

🎧 Listen to “Remaining True to Your Way: A Conversation with Vernon J ’10” on the “Head, Heart, and Hand” podcast.
