Events

Wherever You Are, There's an NMH Event for You

From living rooms to city parks, NMH alumni are gathering in meaningful ways through NMH Local, alumni-led events that bring the NMH spirit to communities around the country and across the world. These gatherings reflect the creativity, generosity, and connection that define our global alumni network.

Alums are also warmly welcomed back to campus throughout the year. Whether it’s a concert, a play, a lecture, or an art exhibit, there’s always something worth experiencing. We especially love seeing you at the Pie Race in the fall, Vespers in the winter, and Reunion in the spring.

We hope to see you soon, on campus or in a community near you.
