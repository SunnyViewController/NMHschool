Host an NMH Local Event

NMH Local events are created by alums, families, and friends for the NMH community. Whether social or professional in nature, these gatherings are a powerful way to bring people together, wherever they are.

From happy hours and Mountain Day hikes to potlucks and Vespers watch parties, past events have taken many shapes. If you have an idea for a gathering, we’d love to help you bring it to life.

  • What Hosts Do
  • How NMH Supports You

What Hosts Do

As an NMH Local host, you’ll take the lead on planning and welcoming your local community. Responsibilities typically include:

  • Choosing a date and time
  • Securing a venue (restaurant, home, park, etc.)
  • Coordinating any vendors (if needed)
  • Drafting basic promotional language
  • Promoting the event on social media and within your networks
  • Serving as the main point of contact for attendees
  • Leading the event on-site (NMH staff do not usually attend)

How NMH Supports You

The NMH Advancement Office is here to make your event a success. We can:

  • Help identify a location and share a list of alumni in the area
  • Create and send event emails with registration links
  • Track RSVPs and keep you informed
  • Promote your event on the NMH Alumni Events Calendar and in Inside NMH

Ready to host or want to learn more?

Reach out to us at alumni@nmhschool.org. We can’t wait to partner with you.
