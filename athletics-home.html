You’ll get the support you need to pursue your athletic goals alongside our rigorous academic program.

You’ll benefit from the flexibility offered in our CMAP program, which allows space for individual workouts and time with our strength and fitness coach in our Calagione Fitness Center. You’ll also enjoy numerous in-season opportunities to compete in tournaments and showcases. 

At NMH, our longstanding commitment to scholar-athletes reflects our belief in nurturing you as a whole person and giving you every opportunity to be your best self.

Making Waves: Paulo ’27 Rewrites the Record Board
Apr 13 2026

Paulo Mota-Neto ’27 delivered a historic season for NMH swimming, breaking long-standing school records in the 50 freestyle (20.71) and 100 freestyle (45.36), the latter standing since 1988. His success, fueled by discipline and dedication, also lifted his teammates—highlighting both individual excellence and the continued rise of the program.

Senior captain Helen Zeaske closes out an exceptional career at NMH as the program’s all-time saves leader, surpassing 2,000 career saves. A four-year cornerstone in net, Zeaske’s impact extends beyond statistics her resilience, leadership, and commitment helped define the culture of NMH girls’ hockey and set the standard for future players.

Olivia Fleming Reaches 1,000 Points
Mar 3 2026

Olivia Fleming ’26 scored her 1,000th career point in a Jan. 28 win over the Kent School, marking a milestone built on years of hard work and leadership at Northfield Mount Hermon. A three-year captain, she credits her teammates and coaches for the achievement and remains focused on pursuing a NEPSAC AA Championship.
