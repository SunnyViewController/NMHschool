Boys Cross Country at NMH

2018 & 2015 NEPSTA Division I Champions

Maybe it's the inspiring wooded foot trails on campus that have spurred NMH's strong tradition in boys' cross-country running: Our teams have won 27 New England titles. Whatever the reason, many runners have gone on to compete at the college and Olympic level, both in cross-country and in track. We compete against the best programs in New England and attend the prestigious Canterbury Invitational each year. Our cross-country team also runs NMH's annual Bemis-Forslund Pie Race, which started in 1908.

Recruiting 
The coaching staff is excited to get to know you! Please email Ggonzalez@nmhschool.org for information and guidance in the application process.
