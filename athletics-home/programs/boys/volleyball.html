Boys Volleyball at NMH

Located near the birthplace of volleyball in Holyoke, Massachusetts, NMH has fielded a boys’ volleyball team for more than thirty years. Both the varsity and junior varsity teams compete during the spring term against public high school teams in western Massachusetts and select independent schools. The program welcomes experienced players and trains athletes new to the sport. Annually, NMH competes in the Founding 4 prep-school tournament.

In the News...

 

Making Waves: Paulo ’27 Rewrites the Record Board

Paulo Mota-Neto ’27 delivered a historic season for NMH swimming, breaking long-standing school records in the 50 freestyle (20.71) and 100 freestyle (45.36), the latter standing since 1988. His success, fueled by discipline and dedication, also lifted his teammates—highlighting both individual excellence and the continued rise of the program.

A Legacy in the Crease: Helen’s Record Breaking Career

Senior captain Helen Zeaske closes out an exceptional career at NMH as the program’s all-time saves leader, surpassing 2,000 career saves. A four-year cornerstone in net, Zeaske’s impact extends beyond statistics her resilience, leadership, and commitment helped define the culture of NMH girls’ hockey and set the standard for future players.

Olivia Fleming Reaches 1,000 Points

Olivia Fleming ’26 scored her 1,000th career point in a Jan. 28 win over the Kent School, marking a milestone built on years of hard work and leadership at Northfield Mount Hermon. A three-year captain, she credits her teammates and coaches for the achievement and remains focused on pursuing a NEPSAC AA Championship.

NMH Celebrates Winter Athletics at Hogspys Ceremony

The event featured team photographs, awards, highlights, and reflections on the winter sports season.

Jimmy Bechter Named National Prep All-American Finalist

Jimmy Bechter ’26 capped off an outstanding three-year career at NMH as a National Prep All-American finalist, solidifying himself as one of the program’s best. Despite a tough finals loss, he reflected on his growth and support system, and now looks ahead to continuing his academic and athletic career at Brown University.

Will Higgs '26 Beyond the Court

After tearing his ACL and missing his junior season, Will Higgs ’26 used a difficult year away from basketball to grow mentally, academically, and personally. With a strong focus on recovery and support from the NMH community, he developed resilience and leadership that have prepared him to succeed at Princeton both on and off the court.

Leading With Passion: Luca Gnazzo’s Impact On and Off the Trails

Luca Gnazzo is already leaving his mark on the NMH mountain biking program. Ranked in the top 10 in a league of 600 riders, Luca combines elite technical skill and relentless work ethic with a genuine passion for the sport.

Lucas Alvan ’26 Named to Brazil’s U20 and Senior World Teams

Lucas Alvan ’26 earned spots on Brazil’s U20 and Senior World Teams after a standout performance at an invitation-only national team tryout in Rio de Janeiro. He will represent Brazil at the Senior Pan American Games in May.

Sisters Thrive on the Field and the Classroom

Livie and Katie Clough have quickly made their mark on NMH Field Hockey. Livie, a junior goalie and team captain, anchors the team with leadership and skill, while sophomore Katie is emerging as a versatile player with Division 1 aspirations. Together, they’ve helped lead NMH to key wins, including a recent victory over Exeter.

Better, Faster, Stronger: NMH Girls' Cross Country Ready to Hit the Trails

The NMH girls’ cross-country team, led by Coach Barry Auskern and senior captain Bryanna May, looks to build on its recent success with a strong, committed roster and a focus on pushing limits this fall.
