The D.L. Moody Prize in Social Entrepreneurship

Recognizing outstanding leaders in the for-profit and nonprofit sectors whose creativity, passion, and commitment make a positive difference in communities around the world 

Established in 2026, the D.L. Moody Prize in Social Entrepreneurship honors the legacy of school founder Dwight L. Moody and celebrates individuals who exemplify the same spirit of innovation, entrepreneurship, and service to others that he modeled in his own extraordinary life. It also recognizes new generations of social entrepreneurs who seek to shape a more equitable, just, and loving world through their commitment to finding innovative solutions to challenges across industries and sectors.
