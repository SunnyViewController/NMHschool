Andrew

Kitchener, Ontario, Canada

 

For his postgraduate year, Andrew sought a school with strong academic and athletics programs. He found that at NMH.

What brought me to NMH:

Basketball brought me to NMH, when the coaching staff first reached out to me I thought it would be a great opportunity to play in the New England Preparatory School Athletic Council. It was important for me in my postgraduate year to find a school that offered strong academics with a good basketball program.
 

The most challenging part of my time at NMH:

Things such as being away from family and balancing the academic schedule with basketball. I handled it very well, as there is a strong supportive community around me here that I was able to go to if needed. 
 

My favorite class:

Postgraduate English, because it challenged me but also I was able to enjoy time off the court with my teammates.
 

Favorite food in Alumni Hall:

Stir fry and the ice cream. 
 

Favorite spot on campus:

The snack bar. I love the chicken quesadillas they make.
 

 

Enjoy each moment, as your time at NMH will go by fast.”

 

Favorite NMH tradition:

I really enjoyed Tron bon. 
 

An educator who’s been especially supportive during my time at NMH:

Both my coaches, Coach Shelton and Coach Jelani. They helped me develop on the court but also helped prepare me to be successful in the future. 
 

Advice I would give myself if I could go back to my first day at NMH:

Enjoy each moment, as your time at NMH will go by fast.
 

How NMH has changed me:

NMH has helped me grow as a student-athlete, as I was able to develop skills like time management and problem-solving. 
 

What’s special to me about NMH:

It has beautiful views of the surrounding hills and trees. It provided me a lot of opportunities in basketball and in the classroom.
 

What I will miss most about NMH:

The friendships I made with my coaches, teammates, and roommates.
 

Where I'm heading next:

Lehigh University
